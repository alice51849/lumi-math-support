Why parents love Lumi Math Planet

FAQ

Frequently asked questions 常見問題

Everything about gameplay, math content, purchase and privacy.

What math does the app cover, and for what ages?涵蓋哪些數學？適合幾歲？+
Designed for ages 5–8. It covers addition & subtraction (including beyond 100, with carrying and borrowing), multiplication tables and division — plus WMI / Math-Kangaroo-style logic: number patterns, reasoning, shapes, spatial sense and counting puzzles. Difficulty scales gently from easy to challenging. 專為 5–8 歲設計。涵蓋加減（含破百三位數、進退位）、乘法表與除法，並有 WMI／袋鼠賽風格的邏輯推理：數列規律、推理、圖形、空間與計數。題目由淺到深自動分級。
Is it a one-time purchase? Any ads or in-app purchases?是一次買斷嗎？有廣告或內購嗎？+
Lumi Math Planet is a one-time paid app. All content is unlocked from the start — there are no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no ads of any kind. 「Lumi 數學星球」是一次買斷的付費 App。所有內容一開始就全部解鎖，沒有任何內購、訂閱或廣告。
Will my child's progress be lost?孩子的進度會不會不見？+
Your planet and progress are saved on the device. They remain as long as the app is installed, and are removed if you delete the app. 星球與學習進度儲存在裝置上，只要 App 還在就會保留；刪除 App 時會一併清除。
How do I use it on a new device?換新裝置怎麼辦？+
Download the app again from the App Store using the same Apple ID — Apple won't charge you again for an app you already own. Note that on-device progress does not transfer automatically between devices. 用原本的 Apple ID 從 App Store 重新下載即可，已購買的 App 不會再次收費。請注意裝置上的進度不會自動跨裝置轉移。
Does it need internet? Does it collect personal data?需要網路嗎？會蒐集個人資料嗎？+
No to both. The app works fully offline and collects no personal data — no accounts, no ads, no third-party tracking. Everything stays on the device. See the privacy policy below. 兩者皆否。App 完全離線運作、不蒐集任何個人資料，無帳號、廣告或第三方追蹤，所有資料都留在裝置上。詳見下方隱私政策。
My child got an answer wrong — is that discouraging?答錯了會不會打擊孩子？+
Never. There are no failures or penalties — a wrong answer simply invites a gentle "try again." The sandbox is built to keep learning calm, positive and pressure-free. 不會。沒有失敗或懲罰，答錯只會溫柔地「再試一次」。沙盒設計讓學習保持平靜、正向、零壓力。

01 What we store, and where

The app stores your child's game progress — the planet they have built, unlocked areas, collected space friends, stars, scores and settings (such as music and voice volume) — only on your device, using on-device local storage. This information is never transmitted to us or to any third party, and it is removed when you delete the app. An optional "export backup" button lets a parent save a copy of this progress as a plain text file; that file is created locally and goes only where you choose to send it (for example, your own Files app). We never receive it. App 僅在裝置本機（on-device local storage）儲存孩子的遊戲進度（已蓋好的星球、解鎖區域、收集的太空朋友、星星、分數，以及音樂／語音音量等設定）。此資料不會傳送給我們或任何第三方，並在你刪除 App 時一併移除。家長可用「匯出備份」按鈕把進度存成一個純文字檔，該檔案在本機產生、只會送到你自己選擇的地方（例如你自己的「檔案」App），我們不會收到。

02 No data collection, no ads, no tracking

Lumi Math Planet does not collect, use, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no advertising, no behavioral profiling, no analytics, no attribution, and no third-party tracking of any kind. Our App Privacy label on the App Store is therefore "Data Not Collected." 「Lumi 數學星球」不蒐集、不使用、不傳輸、不販售、不分享任何個人資料。沒有帳號、沒有登入、沒有廣告、沒有行為分析、沒有數據分析、沒有歸因、也沒有任何第三方追蹤。因此 App Store 上的隱私標籤為「未蒐集資料（Data Not Collected）」。

03 Third-party software we use

The app is built with the open-source Expo and React Native frameworks (including their standard on-device modules for audio playback, text-to-speech, haptics, local storage, and language detection). It contains no advertising, analytics, attribution, social-media, or crash-reporting SDKs — for example, no Google AdMob, no Firebase Analytics, no Facebook SDK, no Sentry. These framework components run locally to display the app and play sound; they do not collect, transmit, or sell your child's personal data, and we have no third-party partners that receive any data from this app. 本 App 以開源的 Expo 與 React Native 框架開發（含其標準的本機模組：音訊播放、語音合成、觸覺回饋、本機儲存、語言偵測等）。不含任何廣告、分析、歸因、社群或當機回報 SDK——例如沒有 Google AdMob、沒有 Firebase Analytics、沒有 Facebook SDK、沒有 Sentry。這些框架元件僅在本機運作以顯示畫面與播放聲音，不會蒐集、傳輸或販售孩子的個人資料；我們也沒有任何會收到本 App 資料的第三方夥伴。

04 Purchases

Lumi Math Planet is a paid app with no in-app purchases. Your purchase is handled entirely by the Apple App Store; we never receive or store your payment information, card number, or Apple ID. 「Lumi 數學星球」為付費、無內購的 App。購買完全由 Apple App Store 處理，我們不會收到或儲存你的付款資訊、信用卡號或 Apple ID。

05 Children's privacy

The app is designed for children (around ages 5 to 8). Because it collects no personal information at all and contains no third-party ads or tracking, it complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR-K. Actions that lead outside the app (such as rating, sharing, or the support link) are placed behind a parental gate. 本 App 為兒童（約 5 到 8 歲）設計。因完全不蒐集個人資訊、且無任何第三方廣告或追蹤，符合美國 COPPA 與歐盟 GDPR-K。任何會離開 App 的動作（評分、分享、支援連結等）都設有家長關卡。

06 Permissions

The app requests no sensitive permissions (no camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or notifications). Audio playback and text-to-speech run entirely on the device. The system Share sheet is only invoked when a parent taps "export backup." App 不要求相機、麥克風、定位、聯絡人、照片或通知等任何敏感權限；音訊播放與語音合成皆於裝置本機運作。只有在家長按下「匯出備份」時，才會叫出系統分享面板。
